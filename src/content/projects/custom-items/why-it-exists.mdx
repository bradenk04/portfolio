---
title: Why it exists
---
Customizing items on a Minecraft server has traditionally required either external plugins with heavy dependencies, manual config editing outside the game, or complicated setup.
Custom Items strips all of that away — no dependencies to hunt down, no tabbing out, just a drag-and-drop jar that works immediately.
It serves any server type, from large RPG worlds with hundreds of named items to small survival SMPs that just need a few tweaks.
